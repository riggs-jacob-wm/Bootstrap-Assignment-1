About Us

Our History

On one brilliant day in 2013, four friends came together with a love for sushi. It was then they came up with the idea for Ristorante con Sushi was born. Featuring four of the most sushi loving people of the world, you know it's going to be good.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to Sushi Gen, a successful chain restaurant that served delicious sushi, but not as delicious as theirs.

Corporate Leadership

Wade "LordMinion777" Barnes Chief Epicurious Officer

Our CEO, Wade, credits his hardworking parents who undertook the arduous journey to the shores of America with the intention of giving their children the best future. He brings his zeal for sushi to this restaurant, pioneering cross-cultural culinary connections.

Seán "Jacksepticeye" McLoughlin Chief Food Officer

Our CFO, Seán, as he is affectionately referred to by his colleagues, comes from a long established family tradition in farming and produce. His experiences growing up on a farm in the Irish outback gave him great appreciation for varieties of food sources. As he puts it in his own words, Everything that runs, wins, and everything that stays, pays!

Bob "Muyskerm" Muyskens Chief Taste Officer

Blessed with the most discerning gustatory sense, Bob, the CFO, personally ensures that every dish that we serve meets his exacting tastes. Our chefs dread the tongue lashing that ensues if their dish does not meet his exacting standards. He lives by his motto, You click only if you survive my lick.

Mark "Markiplier" Fishbach Executive Chefiplier

A man with a huge sushi passion. He thought, Why not make some sushi? And thus, his masterful sushi now blesses the world. People from across the world now come to his restaurant to feast on his fabulous sushi recipes.
